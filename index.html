UBERCHEF

What is UberChef?

UberChef is a service which provides competent chefs to you anytime you need them. Just open the app, pick a menu you like, make an appointment with the chef, and Voila! You've made yourself a full meal. Whether it be a casual meal or for a special occasion.

Take a day off from preparing food. You deserve it. Let Uberchef supply a qualified chef for you. Invite all your friends and family over and don’t worry about a thing. You can organise the day, and choose from anyone of our many chefs and their menus.. And then not worry about a thing.

You can also use our invitation service and payment planner to make everything go easy. Your uber chef will supply the ingredients, use your kitchen to prepare everything, answer all your questions, cook as you wish, serve and clean up. All of our chefs are fully qualified and provide exceptional dining and service. Check out their reviews! Also other services are available at extra cost, such as preparing a few meals in advance for the week, small home classes to learn the tricks, large gatherings can be catered for as well as intimate dinners. Why use Uberchef?

Everyone loves a good dinner party right? It’s a nice easy way to mix in with your colleagues, friends and family. It’s a way of improving existing relationships and reconnecting with those that you care about the most. Our project idea is an easy way to outsource it all. A chef to cook in your own home from a pre-set menu, bringing all ingredients and cleaning up afterwards. This means you can invite more people. This means less stress for you and a great time for everyone.

You could even make money from hosting your own party! Not much, depending on the amount of people. But I was thinking maybe around $100 per head, minimum of six, person whose house it is gets a cashback if everything goes smoothly. So, you are getting a party and meal for free!

Win! Win! For everyone!
